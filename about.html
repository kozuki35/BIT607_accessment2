KAUPAPA

Haukai is a space that supports whanaungatanga (building relationships with others), the opportunity to connect or reconnect with people over beautiful Māori infused kai but most importantly allowing people to create their own story here.

We have a passion for sharing our traditional knowledge of maori kai, in a way that is exciting and accessible to everyone.
